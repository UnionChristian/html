Lenard is frozen solid out of fear as the spider slowly crawled toward him. The spider stopped, then started to wind up for a lunge. Lenard knew he had to fight, so he prepared for one. Then the spider lunged out at him and pinned him down. Lenard couldn't escape, he wasn't devoloped enough to fight! The spider slowly spun a web around him. The last thing he ever saw was the gnarly face of the spider...
